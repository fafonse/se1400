About

What content will this site contain?

 This website will contain various written articles/blogs about art, from creation to appreciation.

Where will this site get this content?

 All written content is created by me (Fabrizio). Art will be sourced from the public domain as well as my own work. AI generated content is not allowed, out of concern for quality and ethics.

Why should users care?

 This site aims to provide a casual perspective. Personally I find a lot of articles online that may describe techniques, but don't teach them in a way that can be applied outside of their medium. However, when looking for more practical techniques online, its easy to stumble upon "hacks", which may teach a technique but don't explain the why the technique works. I hope to bridge the gap between the two, providing comprehensive information with practical applications.

Why should a site be built for this?

 As much as I could publish these articles on blogging sites like Medium and Blogspot, those websites have their own issues. Many blogging services have restricted designs and look outdated in the modern web, and article hosting like Medium tends to drown out anything that isn't self-help or productivity focused. By using my own website, I get both the freedom of design and a stage to myself.

What do you want users to do here?

 Honestly, just reading my articles is enough. I do plan however on adding interactivity, maybe through a comment section, and definitely with a site guestbook. For those wanting to go the extra mile, sharing and referencing my articles with others would be great for increasing my audience.

Whats the goal of this site?

 To help others with their art. When I was younger I remember pouring over articles looking to improve my art, but I mostly found junk articles that rarely offered anything besides a cursory review of a subject. Hopefully this website can provide a more personal touch.
