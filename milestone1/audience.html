Who's the Audience?

 I think that when it comes to my website, most of my audience will be from the USA. I wont specifically try to appeal to Americans, but as an American myself, my writing style will likely appeal to those like me. I don't think this website will appeal to any specific gender, but I think young people willl like it more. I'm young myself, so I can write youthfully I guess.
Outside of that though, I think this website will just attracts artists in general. I want to provide a blog that's at least interesting to artists if not anything else.
