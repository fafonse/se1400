The Competition

 There's a lot of competiton out their for art help websites, from simple blogs to proffessional guides. However, there's a large variance in the quality of the content. Proffessionals often gear their content towards specific mediums and tend to focus on high level concepts, while ignoring the basics. On the other hand, hobbyists like to keep their advice short, but can cut out crucial details about the subject. This website aims to teach high level concepts while providing simplified techniques that can act as stepping stones.
 Articles will focus mostly on digital art, but there will be some articles about tradtional art, specifically around inking and lines. For digital art, topics will range from 3D modeling to photoshop. Digital art is extremely flexible, and multiple mediums intersect at crucial points. To accomadate a challenge, articles will frequently link and reference to each other.
